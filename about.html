Verticle Farming is the future of Urban Farming!

Less Space

Verticle Farming uses the concept of Stack farming. Basically using this approach, we can use the same space of land multiple times.

More Output

Verticle Farming uses the same space of land and produces more Product. Hence less soil yet larger Output.

Organic

Organic farming is a system which avoids or largely excludes the use of synthetic inputs (such as fertilisers, pesticides, hormones, feed, etc.) and to the maximum extent feasible relies upon crop rotations, crop residues, animal manures, off-farm organic waste, mineral grade rock additives and biological system of nutrient mobilisation and plant protection.

Less Water

Unlike in Traditonal Farming, The amount of water wastage is very less. Depending on the moisture levels of the soil, Irrigation is done and in a controlled manner.
